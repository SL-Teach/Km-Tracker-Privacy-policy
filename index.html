Privacy Policy — Driver Log

Last updated: June 2026

Driver Log ("the app") is a vehicle mileage and route tracking application. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Information the app collects

  • Location data (GPS): used to track the distance and route of your drives, including while the app is running in the background (only while a drive is actively being tracked).
  • Bluetooth device information: the app reads the name and address of a Bluetooth device you choose to designate as your car, solely to detect when your phone connects to or disconnects from it, in order to automatically start and stop drive tracking.
  • Information you enter directly: your name and car/vehicle number, which you provide voluntarily in the app's settings.

How this information is used

All of the above is used exclusively to provide the app's core function: recording your drives, calculating distance traveled, and generating mileage reports (PDF/CSV) that you choose to export or share.

Where your data is stored

All trip records, settings, and selected Bluetooth device information are stored locally on your device only. The app does not operate its own server and does not upload your data to any service controlled by the app's developer.

Third-party services used by the app

To provide maps, address lookup, and route directions, the app sends limited data to the following independent, third-party open-source services as needed:

  • OpenFreeMap — provides map tile imagery. Receives the map area being viewed.
  • Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) — provides address search and reverse address lookup. Receives search text or coordinates you query.
  • Project OSRM — provides driving route calculation. Receives the start and end coordinates of a requested route.

These are independent services not operated by the app's developer; their own privacy practices govern data sent to them. No personally identifying information (such as your name) is sent to these services.

Data sharing

The app does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or other third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or tracking tools built into this app.

Your control over your data

You can delete individual trip records at any time within the app (swipe to delete on the History screen). Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the app. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.
